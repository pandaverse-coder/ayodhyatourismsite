Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for international tours and 1-2 months for domestic tours. For peak season travel, booking 4-6 months ahead ensures availability and better pricing.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by tour package. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund, 15-29 days receive a 50% refund, and less than 14 days are non-refundable. Please check specific tour details for exact policies.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all bookings. We partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage for trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and other travel-related issues.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized itineraries tailored to your preferences, budget, and travel style. Contact our travel consultants to discuss your dream vacation and we'll create a personalized package just for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some cases, payment plans can be arranged for larger packages.
